education sectors

Education is a multifaceted sector that involves various components and stakeholders working together to provide comprehensive learning experiences and opportunities. Here are the key sectors involved in education.

56
Public Sector

Government Ministries and Departments: Oversee national education policies, standards, and funding. Public Schools: Primary, secondary, and tertiary institutions funded and managed by the government.

89
Private Sector

Private Schools: Independent schools funded through tuition fees, donations, and private investment. Private Universities and Colleges: Higher education institutions run by private entities. Educational Publishers: Companies that produce textbooks, digital content, and other educational materials.

99
Non-Profit Sector

NGOs and Foundations: Organizations that focus on education-related initiatives, such as scholarships, building schools, and advocacy. Charitable Organizations: Provide funding, resources, and support for education projects. Community Groups: Local organizations that support educational activities and provide resources for students.
